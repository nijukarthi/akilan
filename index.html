ABOUT BRIDEX GLOBAL

The paperwork,
logistics and borders —
handled for you.

Bridex Global sits between origin and destination: we forward freight, clear customs, and manage the compliance trail so a shipment leaving an Indian port lands exactly where — and when — it's supposed to. One point of contact, from booking to bill of lading.

We work as an extension of your supply chain, not a middleman — flagging risk before it costs you a sailing, and keeping every stakeholder reading from the same manifest.

  • Full VisibilityLive shipment status from origin gate to final port of discharge.
  • Compliant by DesignDocumentation built to clear customs the first time, every time.
  • Rate TransparencyFreight and duty costs laid out before you commit to a lane.
  • One DeskA single coordinator across forwarding, clearance and delivery.
