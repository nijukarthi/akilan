WHO WE ARE

Built on a training
institute's network —
run like a trading desk.

Global Exim Sources began life inside a classroom. What started as export-import workshops and seminars grew into one of India's better-known trade training institutes — and that same network of alumni, suppliers and logistics partners became the backbone of a sister company built to trade, not just teach.

Today that network does the sourcing, sorting, packing and shipping, so the commitment behind the original institute — real value for clients, participants and the wider trading community — carries straight through into every consignment we move.

  • Built to CompeteCarry our name or ship white-labelled under yours — the choice is the buyer's.
  • Systems, Not GuessworkOrders run through an automated generation system backed by vendor management software.
  • Full-Service SourcingFrom B2B sourcing to customs clearance, one network handles the whole chain.
  • Client-First PracticesSorting and packaging standards built around what makes each buyer's operation easier.

LEADERSHIP

Thirty years of trade, in one office

Dr. Akilan has worked the export side from nearly every angle — pre-shipment and third-party inspections, export management, and sourcing for overseas buyers — before taking on the Export Promotion Council itself.

That reach carries into Global Exim Sources: a working relationship with traders, suppliers and service providers that extends to DGFT officials and government offices. He remains principal trainer at Global Exim Institute, where new exporters and importers go through live workshops backed by six months of follow-on support.
